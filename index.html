Reasons to Choose Package One

  • Simple and easy to manage with fewer accounts
  • Best for saving budget while still having online presence
  • Good starting point for businesses still testing TikTok
  • Ideal if your focus is on general brand awareness rather than location specific marketing

Limitations of Package One

  • All branches under one TikTok account
  • Audience confusion
  • Hard to give focus to each branch
  • Content becomes general and may not connect with all audiences

Reasons to Choose Package Two

CMC and Bambis share one TikTok account, Mekanisa has its own, GoRedGoRed remains separate

  • Mekanisa branch will have a chance of getting more effort
  • Great option to balance between brand identity and budget

Limitations of Package Two

  • Risk of one branch dominating the content
  • Audience confusion still possible
  • Not ideal for building strong independent brand identity for each location

Reasons to Choose Package Three

Each branch has its own TikTok account

  • Each branch can post content that reflects its specific atmosphere food and customers
  • We can build a loyal following for each location instead of mixing audiences
  • Avoids confusion on the audience
  • Each branch grows at its own pace and we can compare performance between them
